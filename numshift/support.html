NumShift

A number puzzle game where you shift rows and columns to merge tiles that sum to the target. Think ahead, build combos, and climb the levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play NumShift?

Swipe on any row or column to slide the tiles. When adjacent tiles sum to the target number, they merge into a single tile worth that target. Each merge scores points and a new tile spawns. Fill the board with no merges possible and it's game over.

What do the tile colors mean?

Each tile value has its own color — lower values are cool blues and teals, higher values shift toward golds, reds, and purples. Tiles matching the current target have a pulsing glow so you can spot them at a glance.

How do combos work?

Make consecutive merges without a blank move and your combo multiplier climbs: 1.5x after two in a row, 2x after three, and 3x after four or more. The multiplier resets if you make a move with no merge.

How do levels work?

You start at Level 1 with a target of 5. As your score hits certain thresholds, you advance to higher levels with bigger targets (10, 15, 20). Higher levels also introduce tiles with larger starting values.

Can I undo a move?

Yes — tap the Undo button to reverse your last move. You get one undo at a time; it resets after each new move.

Is my data private?

Completely. We collect zero personal data. Your scores and settings are stored locally on your device. See our privacy policy for details.

The app crashed or something looks wrong.

Sorry about that! Please email us with a description of what happened, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots are always helpful. We'll investigate and fix it in the next update.
