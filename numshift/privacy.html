Privacy Policy

Effective: March 29, 2026

TL;DR

Our games collect no personal data. We don't track you, sell your data, or show ads. Your scores stay on your device (or in Game Center if you opt in).

Who We Are

PapaG Studios is an independent game studio. We build puzzle games for iOS. This policy covers all apps published by PapaG Studios on the Apple App Store.

Data We Collect

None. Our games do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no advertising SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

Our games store your preferences (sound, haptics) and local statistics (high score, games played) on your device using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Game Center

Our games may integrate with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. If you are signed into Game Center, Apple handles your Game Center data according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not access your Apple ID, real name, or any personal details through Game Center.

Third-Party Services

Our games do not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share data with anyone because we don't collect any.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our apps are safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect your email, we can't notify you directly — but the current policy is always available here.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@papagstudios.com or visit our support page.
